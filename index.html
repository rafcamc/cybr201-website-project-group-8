Our Mission

Develop robust and resilient artificial intelligence models to detect, withstand, and mitigate emerging AI-driven cyber threats. The TrustSec AI Lab advances research at the intersection of AI and cybersecurity, building AI-driven solutions that enhance the security, robustness, and trustworthiness of interconnected digital systems.

Network Security

Threat detection across complex, distributed environments.

Data Leakage

Preventing sensitive information exposure and model inversion.

Adversarial AI

Attacks, robustness analysis, and certified defenses.

Large Language Models

Risks, attacks, and secure deployment.

Research Focus

Adversarial Robustness

Design and evaluate defense mechanisms to protect AI systems such as fraud detection, risk scoring, and intrusion detection from adversarial attacks.

IoT Privacy in Health

Enhance privacy in health-centric IoT ecosystems using generative models like diffusion for anonymization and differential privacy for rigorous protections.

LLM Hallucination & Privacy

Verify retrieval-augmented generation pipelines and apply secure multi-party computation to reduce leakage and mitigate hallucinations.
